Meet the artist!

HI, i'm chyna *◡*

I don’t have many photos that show my face completely—partly because I’m a shy person and partly because I’m very particular about what I share online. I’m not a coder, but I’m also not a quitter. My website might not be perfect, but it’s the result of my best efforts, guided by what I’ve learned and a lot of Bootstrap magic.

From a young age, my passion for art and drawing has always been a part of me. I was the kind of imaginative kid the kind that drew on the walls of their house (which I got scolded for it). Back then, I was also a huge anime and gaming nerd—interests that played a big role in shaping who I am today. What started as a simple pastime—just me and my sketches—has grown into a full-fledged dream. That’s why I’m here now, studying BMA at FEU Tech, determined to carve my path as a game artist and bring my creative visions to life.

my favorite color is pink ♥

My Hobbies

There is quite a lot that I could list and we could sit here for ages. But for the sake of my project and my time, here are the main four hobbies I'm focusing on.

Digital Drawing

Art is at the core of what I do. I primarily create fan art, often inspired by characters from games. Lately, most of my artworks revolve around two characters from a favorite ship—they’ve become my current muses and continue to fuel my creativity.

Gaming

Gaming is my go-to stress reliever, even though I tend to play competitive games. I’m a laptop gamer and often play with a group of friends. My favorite genre is horror RPGs.

Cosplay

I’ve been cosplaying since I was young, starting with an event at my elementary school. While I don’t attend conventions regularly, I make it a point to participate in at least one each year.

Sewing

I started sewing when I was 18, and my very first project was a dress inspired by Moonlight Cookie from CRK. While I’ve grown to love this hobby, I’ve currently put it on hold to focus on college, as sewing can be quite time-consuming.
